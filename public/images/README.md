# Image Folder

Add your photos here! Replace the placeholder images with your own memories together.

## Recommended Images

Place 4 photos with these names for the parallax sections:
- `photo1.jpg` - Your first adventure together
- `photo2.jpg` - Special moments
- `photo3.jpg` - Fun times and laughter
- `photo4.jpg` - Recent memories

## Image Specifications

For best results:
- **Format**: JPG, PNG, or WebP
- **Aspect Ratio**: 3:4 (portrait) or 4:3 (landscape)
- **Resolution**: At least 800x1000px for portrait, 1200x900px for landscape
- **File Size**: Keep under 500KB each for faster loading

## Tips

1. Choose high-quality, well-lit photos
2. Images with you both in them work best
3. Mix different moments and locations
4. Consider the emotional story you want to tell

The website will automatically display placeholders until you add your photos!
